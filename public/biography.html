Damilola G. Alonge
p/k/a
Mansa Jabulani

Damilola G. Alonge, professionally known as Mansa Jabulani, is a Nigerian music producer, sound engineer, songwriter, and entrepreneur with over a decade of experience in the music industry. Known for his work across Afrobeat, dancehall, and contemporary African sounds, he has contributed to multiple commercially recognized records and collaborated with emerging and established artists within and beyond Nigeria. Beyond production, he has evolved into a music executive focused on building infrastructure for independent artists through Music Distro Entertainment Global Ltd (MDEG), a digital distribution platform designed to support global reach, transparent royalties, and sustainable artist growth. His transition from studio engineering into ecosystem development reflects a broader vision of reshaping how African music is distributed, monetized, and positioned globally. He continues to operate at the intersection of creativity, technology, and music business strategy, driving a long-term shift toward artist ownership and scalable music infrastructure.
